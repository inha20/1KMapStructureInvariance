## 2. Core Discovery

The central mathematical result is:

24 variable arrangements
→ 6 variable partitions
→ 3 representative arrangements

Representative classes:
- AB/CD
- AC/BD
- AD/BC

This result connects variable rearrangement, equivalence classes, symmetry, and D4 transformations.

## 3. Why This Paper Matters

This is not fundamentally a Karnaugh-map technique paper.

The deeper claim is that humans can recognize structural equivalence under representation changes.

Humans do not merely observe patterns.
Humans reorganize representations and discover invariant structure.

The Karnaugh-map case study is evidence for that broader claim.
